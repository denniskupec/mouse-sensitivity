Notes:
There is no way of setting or displaying the actual sensitivty numbers in Payday 2, you can only use the in-game slider. The slider has a total of 420 steps regardless of resolution, and if you use the arrow keys, each click will move it 10%, or 42 steps. So if you for instance want 15% sensitivity, move the slider all the way down, click the right arrow two times, and move the slider back between click 1 and 2 with the mouse, or if you have a steady hand, count 21 steps ("pixels").
