Notes:
Adjust the sensitivity by counting clicks with left/right arrow in the Mouse Options menu. 0 is no bar visible, 19 is the bar full. Disable Vsync to reduce mouse lag/acceleration. There will still be quite a lot of acceleration present. This game has a very narrow sensitivity range, so you probably need to adjust your DPI to accomodate your desired settings.
