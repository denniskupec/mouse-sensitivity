Notes:
Adjust the sensitivity with arrowkeys in-game under Options -> Controls -> Mouse Sensitivity. Each click left or right is 1 step, and the sensitivity bar has 101 steps, ranging from 0-100. So to set a sensitivity of e.g. 9, move the slider all the way to the left, and press the right arrowkey 9 times.
