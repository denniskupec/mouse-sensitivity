Notes:
Vsync must be turned ON. This will make the mouse feel sluggish in the menus etc, but mouse movement is actually much more precise with vsync on. With vsync off these calculations are not correct. You must also use a report rate of 250 Hz or lower. If you use a high report rate, there will be severe negative acceleration.
