Notes:
Adjust the sensitivity with arrowkeys in-game under Options -> Controls -> Sensitivity X and Sensitivity Y. Each click left or right is 10%. So to set a sensitivity of e.g. 55%, move the slider all the way to the left, and press the right arrowkey 6 times, then slide it back between point 5 and 6 with your mouse. Turn off "Reduce Mouse Lag" under Options -> Display -> Advanced Options.
